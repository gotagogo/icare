Services and scope

What advice can be provided on.

Yvonne provides financial advice relating to personal insurance, including (where appropriate): life insurance, health insurance, trauma/medical cover, income protection, mortgage protection, and business/shareholder protection. Advice may include recommendations on setting up new cover, reviewing existing policies, and helping clients understand policy terms and options.

Provider partners

Insurers Yvonne may place business with (not limited to every insurer in the market).

Yvonne commonly works with: AIA, Southern Cross, Partners Life, and Chubb Life. Recommendations will be based on your needs and circumstances and may not include every provider in the market.

Fees, commissions and other benefits

How Yvonne may be paid.

Client fees

In many cases, no direct fee is charged to you for advice. If a fee is payable in your situation, it will be discussed with you and agreed before any work that incurs a fee is undertaken.

Insurer commission / benefits

Yvonne may receive commission from the insurer if you take out a policy, and may receive ongoing commission while the policy remains in force. From time to time, insurers may also offer training, resources, or support. These arrangements are managed under ICARE’s policies.

Conflicts of interest and incentives

How conflicts are identified and managed.

Because commissions may be received, a conflict of interest could arise. ICARE has policies and processes to manage conflicts and ensure advice prioritises clients’ interests. Where relevant, the nature of remuneration and any conflicts will be disclosed as part of your advice process.

Complaints process

How to raise a concern and what happens next.

Step 1: Contact us

If you have a complaint, please email yvonnechen@me.com with the subject line “Complaint”. We will acknowledge and respond within 5 working days.

Step 2: External dispute resolution

If we cannot resolve your complaint, you may contact the external dispute resolution scheme: Financial Services Complaints Limited (FSCL). (You can find their contact details on their website.)

Privacy

How personal information may be used.

To provide advice and arrange insurance, Yvonne may collect and use personal information. Please refer to the Privacy Policy for more details.

Important: The information on this website is general in nature and does not take into account your personal circumstances. Please seek personalised advice before making insurance decisions.

服務內容與範圍

可提供哪些保險相關理財建議。

Yvonne 提供與個人保險相關的理財建議，視個人狀況可能涵蓋： 人壽保險、健康保險、重大疾病／創傷與醫療保障、收入保障、房貸保障，以及企業／股東保障。 建議內容可能包含：新保障規劃、既有保單檢視、條款說明與方案比較等。

合作保險公司

可能協助投保的保險公司（不代表涵蓋市場上所有保險公司）。

Yvonne 常見合作的保險公司包含：AIA、Southern Cross、Partners Life、Chubb Life。 建議將以你的需求與個人狀況為基礎，並不一定包含市場上所有保險公司。

費用、佣金與其他利益

Yvonne 可能如何收取報酬。

客戶費用

多數情況下，客戶不需直接支付諮詢費用。若在你的情況下需要收費，會在產生任何需付費的工作前， 先與你說明並取得同意。

保險公司佣金／其他支持

若你投保成功，Yvonne 可能會向保險公司收取一次性與續期（ongoing）佣金。 有時保險公司也可能提供教育訓練、資源或支持；這些安排會依 ICARE 的政策進行管理。

利益衝突與誘因管理

如何識別與管理利益衝突。

因可能收取佣金，可能產生利益衝突。ICARE 設有相關政策與流程，用以管理利益衝突並確保建議優先考量客戶利益。 若與你的建議相關，將在理財建議流程中揭露報酬方式與可能的利益衝突。

申訴流程

如何提出申訴，以及後續處理方式。

步驟 1：先與我們聯絡

若你要提出申訴，請 email 至 yvonnechen@me.com， 主旨請註明「Complaint／申訴」。我們將在 5 個工作天 內回覆。

步驟 2：外部爭議處理機構

若無法與我們達成解決方案，你可聯絡外部爭議處理機構： Financial Services Complaints Limited（FSCL）。 （你可至 FSCL 官方網站查詢其聯絡方式。）
