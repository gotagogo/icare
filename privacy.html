1. Scope

Who this policy applies to.

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through this website and through communications with Yvonne Chen (for example via email, phone, WeChat, or during the advice and application process).

2. Information we may collect

Types of information we may need.

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, address)
  • Information you provide when asking for advice (for example family situation, financial commitments, cover needs)
  • Insurance-related information required to arrange cover or support claims (may include health information where relevant)
  • Website usage information (limited technical data such as device/browser and pages viewed)

3. How we use your information

What we do with the information collected.

  • To respond to enquiries and communicate with you
  • To provide financial advice and explain insurance options
  • To help apply for insurance and liaise with insurers where needed
  • To support policy reviews and claims assistance
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations in New Zealand

4. Who we may share information with

Where sharing is necessary.

We may share your information with relevant parties where necessary to provide services, such as:

  • Insurers and underwriting/claims teams (to arrange cover or support claims)
  • Service providers who support business operations (for example document storage, email, or IT services)
  • ICARE (the Financial Advice Provider) where required for compliance and oversight
  • Regulators or dispute resolution bodies where required by law or for complaint handling

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Storage and security

How we protect information.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration. Information may be stored electronically and/or in physical form, and access is limited to authorised persons where possible.

6. Retention

How long information may be kept.

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal or regulatory requirements.

7. Access and correction

Your rights.

You may request access to, or correction of, your personal information. To do so, please contact yvonnechen@me.com.

8. Complaints

If you have concerns about privacy.

If you believe your privacy has been breached, please contact Yvonne first so we can investigate and respond. If you are not satisfied, you may raise your complaint through the applicable external processes as described in the Disclosure Statement.

9. Updates

Changes to this policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page.

Important: The information on this website is general in nature and does not take into account your personal circumstances. Please seek personalised advice before making insurance decisions.

1. 適用範圍

本政策適用於哪些情境。

本隱私權政策適用於透過本網站蒐集的資訊，以及你與 Yvonne Chen 之間的溝通與服務過程 （例如 email、電話、WeChat、理財建議與投保流程等）。

2. 我們可能蒐集的資訊

為提供服務可能需要的資訊類型。

  • 聯絡資訊（姓名、email、電話、地址等）
  • 你在諮詢時提供的資訊（例如家庭狀況、財務承擔、保障需求）
  • 為協助投保或理賠所需的保險相關資訊（視情況可能包含健康資訊）
  • 網站使用資訊（有限的技術資料，例如裝置/瀏覽器與瀏覽頁面）

3. 我們如何使用你的資訊

蒐集資訊的用途。

  • 回覆詢問並與你聯繫
  • 提供理財建議並說明保險選項
  • 協助投保並在需要時與保險公司溝通
  • 支援保單檢視與理賠協助
  • 遵循紐西蘭法律與監管要求

4. 我們可能與誰分享資訊

在必要情況下的分享。

為提供服務，我們可能在必要範圍內與相關單位分享你的資訊，例如：

  • 保險公司與核保／理賠團隊（用於投保或理賠處理）
  • 支援營運的服務供應商（例如文件儲存、email 或 IT 服務）
  • ICARE（Financial Advice Provider），用於合規與監督需求
  • 依法要求或處理申訴/爭議時可能涉及的監管機構或爭議處理單位

我們不會販售你的個人資訊。

5. 儲存與安全

我們如何保護資訊。

我們採取合理措施，保護你的個人資訊免於遺失、濫用、未經授權的存取、揭露或更改。 資訊可能以電子或紙本形式保存，並盡可能限制只有授權人員可存取。

6. 保存期間

資訊可能保存多久。

我們僅在提供服務及符合法律/監管要求所需期間內保存個人資訊。

7. 查閱與更正

你的權利。

你可要求查閱或更正你的個人資訊。請聯絡 yvonnechen@me.com。

8. 申訴

若你對隱私處理有疑慮。

若你認為你的隱私遭到侵害，請先聯絡 Yvonne 以便我們調查並回覆。 若你對處理結果仍不滿意，可依 揭露聲明 中所述流程進一步提出申訴。

9. 政策更新

本政策可能變更。

我們可能不定期更新本隱私權政策；最新版本將發布於此頁面。

提醒： 本網站內容僅供一般資訊參考，未考量你的個人狀況。做出保險決策前，請先取得個人化建議。
