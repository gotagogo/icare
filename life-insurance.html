Common questions

A few quick answers to help you get started.

Do I need life insurance if I’m single?

It depends. Some people want cover for debts, future family plans, or to protect business obligations. A quick chat helps clarify.

How much cover should I have?

There isn’t one “right” number. We usually look at living costs, debts, dependants, and timeframes—then match it to budget.

Will it cost more if I wait?

Premiums are often influenced by age and health. Planning earlier can give you more options, but we’ll work with where you’re at.

Can I review later?

Absolutely. Yvonne supports ongoing reviews as your life changes—new home, new baby, new business, or updated priorities.

常見問題

先用幾個重點，幫你快速建立方向。

單身也需要人壽保險嗎？

需要與否取決於你的狀況。有些人會為了貸款、未來家庭規劃，或企業責任而提前安排。簡單聊一下就能更清楚。

保障額度要抓多少？

沒有一個適用所有人的固定答案。我們通常會看生活費、負債、需要照顧的人，以及希望支撐的時間長度，再對應預算規劃。

晚點再買會更貴嗎？

保費往往與年齡與健康狀況有關。越早規劃通常選擇更多，但我們也會以你目前的狀況為基礎，找出可行方案。

之後可以再檢視調整嗎？

當然可以。買房、生小孩、換工作或創業後，都很適合做保單檢視。Yvonne 也會提供長期支持與更新建議。
