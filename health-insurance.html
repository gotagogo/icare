Common questions

A few quick answers to help you feel confident.

Is health insurance worth it in NZ?

Many people choose it for flexibility and peace of mind—especially around private treatment pathways. The best fit depends on your priorities, budget, and existing arrangements.

What should I watch for when comparing plans?

Pay attention to policy wording, annual limits, exclusions, and waiting periods. Yvonne can walk you through the fine print in plain language.

Can I change providers later?

Often yes, but it’s important to consider continuity, underwriting, and how pre-existing conditions are treated. We’ll review the options carefully before you make changes.

What if I already have cover through work?

Great—let’s review what it includes and what it doesn’t. Some people keep it as a base and add personal cover for better control and stability.

常見問題

先用幾個重點，幫你建立清楚概念。

在紐西蘭，健康保險值得嗎？

不少人選擇健康保險，是因為想要更彈性的就醫選擇與安心感（尤其私立醫療流程）。 是否適合，仍取決於你的需求、預算與既有安排。

比較方案時要注意哪些重點？

建議留意條款用語、年度額度上限、除外責任、以及等待期等。 Yvonne 可以用白話協助你把差異看懂。

之後可以更換保險公司嗎？

通常可以，但需要留意保障延續性、核保結果，以及既有疾病（pre-existing conditions）可能帶來的影響。 我們會先完整評估，再做出合適決定。

如果公司有團體保險，還需要另外買嗎？

很好——我們可以先檢視團體保險涵蓋內容與限制。 有些人會把團體保險當作基礎，再搭配個人保障以提升可控性與穩定性。
